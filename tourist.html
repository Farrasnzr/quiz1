Alun-Alun Bangil

Alun-Alun Bangil, tempat berkumpulnya warga lokal dan wisatawan

Alun-Alun Bangil is a popular public square located in the heart of Bangil, Pasuruan. It serves as a central gathering place for both locals and tourists. The square is surrounded by various shops, cafes, and street food vendors, making it a lively spot to experience the local culture. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks, relax on benches, or participate in community events that are often held here. The Alun-Alun is especially vibrant during weekends and holidays when families come to spend quality time together.

Masjid Agung Bangil

Masjid Agung Bangil, salah satu masjid besar di Bangil

Masjid Agung Bangil is one of the largest and most significant mosques in Bangil. It is known for its stunning architecture, which combines traditional Javanese and Islamic design elements. The mosque features a spacious prayer hall, beautiful domes, and intricate carvings that showcase the craftsmanship of local artisans. It serves as a spiritual center for the Muslim community in Bangil and attracts visitors who come to admire its beauty and participate in religious activities. The mosque also plays an important role during Islamic festivals and events.
