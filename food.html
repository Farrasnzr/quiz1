Punel

Nasi punel, yang disajikan dengan lauk pelengkap

Nasi punel is a typical dish from Bangil, Pasuruan, called "Punel" because it comes from the Javanese language which means "Penuh" or "Berlimpah". This food is famous for its large portions. The name Nasi punel also comes from the Javanese word "pulen" which means cooked just right, not too dry, and not too soft. The texture of Nasi pulen is soft and slightly lumpy. Generally, this food is served on a plate lined with banana leaves. On top of the rice, sprinkled serundeng, accompanied by mussel satay, lentho/menjeng, Balinese spiced tofu, sliced ​​meat and tendon, and a small packet of gravy containing grated coconut with coconut milk and given a slightly sweet seasoning.Usually, there are bamboo shoots, lodeh and young jackfruit vegetables. The main side dishes are empal, fried chicken, omelet, lung, and beef jerky, as well as spicy ground chili sauce mixed with sliced ​​long beans.

Karakteristik & Komponen Nasi Punel

Aspek Keterangan
Tekstur nasi Lembut dan sedikit menggumpal — agar terasa pulen.
Proses memasak Beras dicuci, direndam sebentar, lalu ditanak. Setelah matang diaduk agar lebih padat dan pulen.
Penyajian Biasanya menggunakan alas daun pisang untuk menambah aroma tradisional.
Lauk & pelengkap

Beberapa lauk yang sering menyertai:

  • Serundeng (parutan kelapa berbumbu)
  • Sate kerang
  • Lentho / menjeng (olahan ikan)
  • Tahu bumbu Bali
  • Irisan daging & kikil
  • Sayur seperti rebung, lodeh, nangka muda
  • Sambal ulek & sambal terasi kacang panjang
