Welcome to Bangil, Pasuruan.

Bangil is a town and district in Pasuruan Regency, East Java, Indonesia. It is located about 65 kilometers southeast of Surabaya, the capital city of East Java. Bangil is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and traditional markets. The town has a mix of modern amenities and traditional Javanese culture, making it an interesting place to visit. Some notable attractions in Bangil include the historic Bangil Mosque, traditional markets like Pasar Baru, and various local eateries that offer authentic Javanese cuisine. Bangil also serves as a gateway to other tourist destinations in East Java, such as Mount Bromo and the beaches along the southern coast.
